Services FAQ

How do I choose between Botox and Jeuveau?
Both soften expression lines; your injector will recommend the right option during your consult based on your goals.
What’s the downtime for SkinPen microneedling?
Most patients are pink for 24–48 hours with light flaking; makeup is typically fine the next day.
How many laser hair removal sessions will I need?
A series is typical—many see results within 6–8 sessions depending on area, hair, and skin type.
Do you serve both Westfield and Carmel?
Yes—book at either location; we also serve Zionsville, Fishers, and North Indy.
