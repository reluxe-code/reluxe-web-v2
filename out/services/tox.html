Tox (Neurotoxin Wrinkle Removers)

At RELUXE, we offer the full lineup of wrinkle relaxers—Botox®, Jeuveau®, Dysport®, and Daxxify®—because different faces (and lifestyles) benefit from different options. All four soften expression lines like frown lines, forehead lines, and crow’s feet by relaxing the tiny muscles that create creases. Treatments are quick with little to no downtime, results start to appear in a few days, and for some people Daxxify can last longer, while Dysport may kick in a touch sooner. Botox is the classic, ultra-reliable choice; Jeuveau is loved for a smooth, aesthetic finish. We truly like them all.

Your visit starts with a friendly consult where we map your expressions, discuss goals, budget, and timeline (big events, maintenance pace), and help you choose the right tox for you—sometimes even a thoughtful combo. We customize dosing for natural, balanced results (never “frozen”), keep you comfortable, and share simple aftercare. You’ll leave with a clear plan, a two-week check-in if needed, and an easy schedule for touch-ups so you stay “you, just smoother.”

Top questions about Tox (Neurotoxin Wrinkle Removers)

Will I look frozen?

No—our approach preserves natural movement while softening lines.

When do I see results?

Most start seeing results in 2–7 days; full effects by day 14.

How long does it last?

Typically 3–4 months (Daxxify may last longer for some).

Does it hurt?

Quick pinches; most rate it 2–3/10. Numbing optional.

Can I work out?

Wait 24 hours to avoid migration.
