Botox

Botox® is a quick, no-downtime treatment that softens frown lines, forehead lines, and crow’s feet so you look rested—not “frozen.” Tiny injections relax the overactive muscles that create creases, with improvements appearing in a few days and full results at about two weeks. Most people describe it as a few small pinches, and then they’re back to work, errands, or the gym.

At RELUXE, your visit starts with a friendly, honest consult. We map your unique expressions, discuss goals and budget, and create a plan that keeps your features natural and balanced. You’ll leave with simple aftercare tips and a clear timeline for touch-ups (typically every 3–4 months, depending on your muscles and metabolism).

Top questions about Botox

What is Botox?

Botox is an FDA-approved injectable that relaxes targeted muscles to smooth wrinkles and prevent new ones from forming.

Will I look frozen?

No—our approach preserves natural movement while softening lines.

When do I see results?

You’ll start to notice changes in 3–5 days, with full results in about 2 weeks

How long does it last?

Typically 3+ months, but it can vary for each person.

Does it hurt?

Quick pinches; most rate it 2–3/10.

Is there any downtime?

No major downtime — you can return to normal activities right after, but avoid exercise for 24 hours and lying flat or rubbing the area for 4 hours.
