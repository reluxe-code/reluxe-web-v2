Your Wedding Prep Timeline

We personalize every step to your date, goals, and skin. Here’s a proven structure to stay stress-free.

6+ Months

  • Consultation & custom plan
  • Laser hair removal series (face, underarms, bikini, legs)
  • SkinPen® microneedling for texture/acne scars
  • Begin medical-grade skincare (Skinbetter, SkinCeuticals, Universkin)

3–4 Months

  • Neurotoxins: Botox®, Dysport®, Jeuveau®, Daxxify®
  • Filler for lips/cheeks/chin/jawline (as needed)
  • EvolveX body contouring (tone, tighten)
  • Glow-building facials: Glo2Facial®, HydraFacial®

1–2 Months

  • Opus Plasma®, ClearLift, or IPL for refinement
  • Final filler tweaks (if needed)
  • Spruce up brows & lashes; maintain skincare
  • Massage therapy for stress relief

Final 2–3 Weeks

  • Signature facial + dermaplane
  • Hydrinity hydration boost
  • Subtle tox touch-ups (timed for photos)
  • Recovery buffers before events

Wedding Prep FAQs

When should I start wedding prep?
We recommend starting 6+ months out for best results, with a focused push 3–4 months before. We can still help on tighter timelines—book a consult and we’ll prioritize high-impact treatments.
How close to the wedding can I get injectables?
Most brides and grooms plan tox 3–4 weeks prior so results settle before photos. Filler timing depends on the area; we’ll advise based on your goals and date.
Can we book treatments for the whole wedding party?
Yes—ask about bridal party bundles, tox parties, and same-day glow facials. We can host private events at Carmel or Westfield.
What if I have sensitive skin?
We’ll tailor a gentle regimen, leverage Skinbetter/Colorescience/Hydrinity, and plan test-drives so there are no surprises close to the big day.
